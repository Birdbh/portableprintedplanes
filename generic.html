Mark I
Atlas

Our very first ultra light plane. Aspect Ratio and Chord Length are optimized for glider performance.
Actuated to achieve maximum flight time and equilibrium position.
Atlas marks the advent of a dream to bring portable planes to the world

Atlas is truly a State of the Art development. It represents a leap forward in the realm of personal avionics. Its free form structure and low drag volume allows for optimum flow below wings across the body. It is equipped with a classical airfoil and circular support structures as well as a practice fuselage system. Its greatest strength lies in the optimization of airflow between central body pins and vertical wing interface.

The Modular nature of Atlases components allow for ease of assembly and lean development. In the case of hazard or broken components acquisition and replacement can occur at minimal cost. Flight Data can be found below, along with wind tunnel video.
